Welcome to the Art Gallery

About the Gallery

The Art Gallery is dedicated to preserving and promoting the diverse art traditions of India. From ancient sculptures to modern paintings, our collection offers a glimpse into India's artistic heritage.

Our Collection

Madhubani Art

Traditional art from Bihar, known for its vibrant colors and intricate patterns.

Warli Art

A tribal art form from Maharashtra, characterized by its monochromatic depictions of daily life.

Tanjore Art

A classical South Indian painting style, noted for its use of gold leaf and vivid colors.

Pattachitra Art

One of the oldest and most popular art forms of Odisha, featuring mythological narratives.

Miniature Art

Delicate and detailed art form that flourished during the Mughal era, showcasing royal scenes.
